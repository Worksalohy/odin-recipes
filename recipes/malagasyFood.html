Sausages and Watercress

Descriptions

This sausage and watercress is a Malagasy food. They usually eat it as a main course during lunch. And always accompanied by hot rice.

Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Wathercress
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Rice
  • 500 ml of wather
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare each Ingredient
  2. Cook rice
  3. Cook the sausages, tomatoes and onions thogether.
  4. After 20 minutes, watercress is added to the saussages.
  5. We wait 40 minutes and the saussage and watercress is ready
